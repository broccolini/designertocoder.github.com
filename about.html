Zach Holman

You can find Mr. Holman on Twitter or send him an email.

Mr. Holman currently builds internet at GitHub. He leads development on Firewall Install, a private GitHub for your clearly intelligent and forward-thinking company.

For the last decade, Mr. Holman has also crafted Good-Tutorials, which has amassed such a darling display of design and programming tutorials that it's advised not to look directly into the page for fear of going blind.

He also got a lot of exposure and press for Facelette, a ridiculous experiment. You may be interested in reading his post about it.

Mr. Holman enthusiasticly resides in San Francisco, California. He does miss his hometown of Fargo, North Dakota, and severely misses Pittsburgh, Pennslyvania, where he attended Carnegie Mellon University.

If you have any questions or feedback about Mr. Holman, check out the issues on holman/feedback on GitHub. It's sort of like an "Ask Me Anything" setup, except it uses a nerdy social network and crams it into a bug tracker.
